# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

AgriDoc is built for Indian farmers. We are committed to making this community welcoming to contributors of all backgrounds, languages, and technical levels.

## Standards

**Positive behavior:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Respecting differing viewpoints, especially from non-English speakers
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for farmers and rural communities

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, trolling, or discriminatory comments
- Dismissing contributions because of language barriers
- Publishing others' private information

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org), version 2.1.
